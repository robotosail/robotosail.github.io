R's Snake Game

This is just another version of the snake game but with a twist. You can not only reset the game, but it using R's specially function, you are able to customize your controls to whatever you feel is comfortable. The controls are wasd or arrow keys.

R's Shooter Game

You click in the position of the enemies to kill them. The controls are wasd to move and left click to shoot
